Gamify Unplugged: How Gamifying Surveys can Reduce Cognitive Load

In today's digital age, it is important for both users and developers to understand the relationship between cognitive load and productivity. Cognitive load refers to the amount of mental energy required to interpret and complete a task. Learning how to reduce cognitive load can improve user productivity, help deal with short attention spans, and encourage the retention of information—essential for new technology and the completion of day-to-day tasks.

One way to minimize cognitive load is through gamification—the act of incorportating game-like elements into non-game settings. To further understand this idea, please take the survey below!
